My Story

Meet the Photographer

I'm Hannah - a part-time wedding and family photographer based in Broughton, North Lincolnshire. I cover the local area and am always happy to travel for weddings.

I have always enjoyed taking photos, and after having my daughter Poppy in 2018 I decided to turn it into a proper business. Photography fits around family life and lets me do something I genuinely enjoy. I keep things simple - no fussy posing, just natural shots that capture people as they are. I pay attention to the details that matter and take pride in delivering photos people actually want to look at.

It has grown steadily over the years into a warm, accessible business with over 200 five-star reviews and a community of families and couples who trust me with their most precious days.

I've been honoured to win Wedding Photographer of the Year (Northern England) via Lux Life Magazine, rated Top 3 Photographer in North Lincolnshire, and received an AFNS Award in May 2021. But what means most is knowing my couples and families love their photos.

I'd love to hear from you. Whether you're planning a wedding near or far, hoping to book a family session, or just want to say hello - my inbox is always open.

What I Offer

Full-day coverage from getting-ready to the dance floor. I'll be there to capture every tear, laugh, and spontaneous moment - with a relaxed, documentary-style approach that feels like having a friend with a camera.

Family Photography

Outdoor sessions in beautiful natural settings - woodland paths, golden beach light, open countryside. No stiff posing, just your family being yourselves. These sessions are relaxed, unhurried, and full of genuine connection.

  • Outdoor natural settings

  • All digital files included
  • Print-ready gallery
